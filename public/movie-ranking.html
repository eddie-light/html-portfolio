The Best Movies According to Eddie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

An all-time classic. Marlon Brando and Al Pacino are fantastic in this film.

I'm Still Here

An extraordinary Brazilian film based on a true story from the period Brazil underwent a military dictatorship. Fernanda Torres, the main character, has been nominated for Best Actress at this years Oscar Awards.

Duck Soup

A classic comedy by the Marx Brothers who arguably rank as the best comedians ever.
